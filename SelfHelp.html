Self Improvement How-To's

Mindfulness and Meditation

  • Commands thoughts and actions to shift from limbic system- the emotional and fear aspect of your brain- to your conscious awareness in the frontal lobe.

  • Due to neuroplasticity, the connection between these two sections of the brain strengths and allows for your frontal lobe to gain control over limbic system, making you calmer and more emotionally strong.

Yoga

  • Impacts memory and emotions: amygdala- the area that contributes to emotional regulation- can expand when you regularly practice yoga, meaning your ability to feel and perceive emotions will grow.

  • The prefrontal cortex can also expand, meaning you’ll be more prone to making the ‘right’ decisions and thinking through things.

Positive Outlook and Self Acceptance

  • Can improve immune system, your ability to cope with stress, making you emotionally agile.

  • Cognitive broadening: range of the resources developed through your brain and body by positive emotions. This means the more positive thoughts and affirmations we feed our brain, our cognitive thinking broadens and we develop more positive emotions and sense of possibilities in our mind—> allow us to build skills, resources, and find value in other areas of our lives.

  • Negative emotions, on the other hand, tend to make our brains believe that these positive outlooks and hopes are irrelevant and are a threat to us.
